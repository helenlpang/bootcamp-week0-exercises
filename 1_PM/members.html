Running Man Members

The original members of Running Man are Yoo Jae-suk, Ji Suk-jin, Kim Jong-kook, Gary, Haha, Lee Kwang-soo, and Song Joong-ki. Song Ji Hyo joined the cast in the 6th episode. In April 2011, Song Joong-ki recorded his last episode (episode 41) in May, and left the program to focus on his acting career. Lizzy was a cast member from episodes 18 to 26. On October 25, 2016, Gary left the show to focus on his music career after being with Running Man for 6 years. On April 3, 2017, Jeon So-min and Yang Se-chan joined the cast.
