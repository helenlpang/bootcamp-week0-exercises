Running Man Guests

Running Man has had a large amount of guests participate in missions and episodes. During its run, some of the guests were known for their constant appearances and were often considered to be the "8th/9th/10th Running Man". The following list are guests who appeared the most often as of 29 December 2019.

  1. Kang Ha Na
  2. Hong Jin Young
  3. Lee Sang Yeob
  4. Son Na Eun
  5. Jung Yong Hwa
  6. Lee Da Hee
  7. Nichkhun
  8. Yoon Bo Mi
  9. Heo Kyung Hwan
  10. Park Ji Sung
  11. Seung Ri
  12. Suzy
